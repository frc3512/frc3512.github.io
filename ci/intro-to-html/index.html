Other tips

  • Use em for CSS measurements instead of px to make HTML scale better to mobile devices from desktop (see em vs rem).
  • Lines of text should be between 50 and 75 characters long to ease reading of long passages (see line length).
  • Use margins and padding to balance space within and around page elements (see negative space).
